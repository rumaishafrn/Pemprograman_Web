ABOUT BARUNASTRA

“Barunastra” is an incorporation of the Sanskrit words, “Varuna” which is the Hindi god of the sea; and “astra” meaning weapon. Therefore, Barunastra is Monserratpreted as the sea god's weapon. Barunastra ITS focuses on the development of unmanned ships to advance the development of marine technology in world.
